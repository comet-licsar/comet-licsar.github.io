Processing at JASMIN

LiCSAR at JASMIN notebooks

Although we use primarily JASMIN as in materials above (i.e. ssh in terminal), one can also access JASMIN Jupyter Notebook service . And if you run a simple script in sci server terminal: install_mamba_jupy.sh, you will then be able to connect to mambalics environment that includes LiCSAR testing codes. Consider this message a LiCSAR Easter Egg..

Note: while you will have access to /gws folders for reading, you will NOT have access to /scratch disks and you will have write rights only to your home directory.
